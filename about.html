About Us

At Pollen Nation, we create beautiful landscapes that support the environment and help people connect with nature. We specialize in ecological landscaping, native plants, pollinator gardens, food gardens, backyard beehives, and small farm or homestead support—designed for Colorado’s Front Range and built to work with nature, not against it.

About the Owner

Pollen Nation is owned and operated by Camden, a Colorado State University graduate with a Bachelor’s degree in Biology, a concentration in Botany, and a minor in Horticulture. After graduating, Camden spent three years working in the landscaping industry, where he refined his skills and learned how to integrate scientific plant knowledge into the practice and craft of landscape design.

During this time, he recognized that much of the green industry relies on chemical-heavy practices that perpetuate a cycle of poor plant selection, synthetic inputs, depleted soils, and landscapes that merely survive rather than thrive. In response, Camden pursued the certifications, licensing, and insurance required to build a landscaping company rooted in ecological integrity—one focused on restoring soil health, supporting plant resilience, and proliferating life.
